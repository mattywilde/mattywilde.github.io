About Me

Background

From my early days creating YouTube videos with friends—synchronizing in-game sound effects to the beat of songs—my interest in music technology was sparked. One of my favorites involved syncing gunshots from Fortnite to Daft Punk's Harder Better Faster Stronger in 2018.

Education & Focus

I pursued a degree in Sound Engineering and Production, where I delved into both recording techniques and the scientific/software aspects of audio processing. Whilst I originally planned to focus just on recording and mixing, I soon realised my passion spans everything from machine learning to radio broadcasting. This fusion of computers and music technology led me to specialize in digital signal processing and audio plugin development.

Skills & Projects

  • Audio Plugin Development (JUCE, C++)
  • Wavelet-Based Noise Reduction & Transient Removal
  • Game Audio Design (Unity, FMOD, Unreal, Wwise)
  • DSP Algorithms: Filters, Dynamics Processing, Spectral Analysis
  • Machine Learning for Audio (Onset Detection, Pitch Tracking)

Inspirations

Video game music and sound design from franchises like Drakengard & Nier and the immersive audio landscapes of Grand Theft Auto and Red Dead Redemption have profoundly influenced my creative approach.

Favorites & Hobbies

  • Fav Artists: Daft Punk, Magdalena Bay, M83, MGMT, Charli XCX, yeule, Grimes
  • Hobbies: Gaming, Reading Sci-Fi & Fantasy, Football
  • Currently Exploring: Creating a small horror game
